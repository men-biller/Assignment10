The difference between EF4 and EF5 damage in meteorology.

By Benjamin Miller

The difference in damage as seen between the Joplin EF5 tornado and the Rolling Fork-Silver City EF4 tornado. In this page, I have demonstrated my ability to rotate an image, as well as hide the image of Joplin to bring emphasis to the more recent twister, the 2023 Rolling Fork-Silver City EF4. This page exists to showcase how much of a difference EF4 is from EF5 damage.
